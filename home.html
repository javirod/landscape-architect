LANDSCAPE ARCHITECT

As a Landscape Architect, I value interdisciplinary communication and collaboration. I believe that by working together, professionals from different backgrounds can make a bigger impact than they can by themselves. I trust that through design and engagement, Landscape Architects have the power to protect underserved communities, invigorate native ecologies, and design outstanding human-centric outdoor environments and I look forward to being art of these solutions.
